    Some of the physical signs of opiate addiction and abuse may include:
  • Someone who is abusing opioids may be noticeably euphoric or extremely happy after taking a dose of a drug. Once the sense of euphoria subsides, another physical symptom of opiate abuse is often a feeling of sedation or tiredness. Confusion
  • Pupils that are constricted.
  • Nodding off at random times or loss of consciousness.
  • Slower breathing rate.
